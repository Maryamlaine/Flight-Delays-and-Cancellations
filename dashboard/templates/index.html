Project Details

For this project, we looked at flight delays for airports in the United States during March 2019 and March 2020. This was chosen to see what impact COVID-19 may have had compared to the previous year.

We utilized data from the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, which were uploaded to a postgresSQL database hosted by AWS. For our backend, we used Python for ETL, and for the frontend developed a Flask app, utilizing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the following JS libraries: D3, Plotly, & Leaflet. This was then deployed on the Heroku cloud platform.

The Flight Paths within US Airlines

This plot uses edge bundling to show airline flight routes, with thicker edges corresponding to longer delays.

Bubble Chart
