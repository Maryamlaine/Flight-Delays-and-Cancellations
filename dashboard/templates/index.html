Project Details: SUBJECT TO CHANGE!

Our goal was to create a dashboard of three data visualizations on airline flight delays across the United States. First, we wrangled data from both kaggle.com and the Bureau of Transportation Statistics. With this data, we visualized flight routes for each airline using a Voronoi Arc Map package for D3.

Next, we plotted a Calendar Heatmap in order to show which days of the month saw the highest number of delays throughout the year. ### For our last plot: arrival vs. departure delays & Bubble Chart ### What to add here?
